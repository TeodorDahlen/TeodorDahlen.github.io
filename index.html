Tool overview

As i love making games, all aspects of it. I have some knowladge in all parts of game creation.

  • Unity C#

    Have worked with unity on and of ever seanse i started studing at LBS in Varberg 2018. I have explored many of its features and like finding new workflows. Making anything from small games to more large scale projects

  • Unreal engine

    Started working with Unreal engine at YRGO. Blueprints is a fantastic tool if you want to quickly explore and test features. The Niagara visual effects system is something i have spent a great amount of time exploring

  • Maya

    I have been using Autodesk Maya since I began exploring game development, and I feel confident working with its fundamental tools, workflows, and navigation

  • Photoshop

    Being able to change sprites, edit pictures and or anything that dont require an true artist eyes is something that have saved me the most time in game develpoment

  • Unity & UE5 Material Editor

    I can quickly achieve the desired result and truly enjoy the process. I wouldn’t call myself an artist, but if I know my goal, I can replicate it effectively.

  • Unity & UE5 VFX tools

    When I get stuck in programming, I find it refreshing to explore visual effects. Without strong VFX, a game can often feel empty or unfinished.
